About

As mentioned above our studio operates out of Collierville, TN at a local Church, conveniently located within 5 miles of Huston High School and Collierville High School.

Students who wish to take lessons through our studio will receive a personalized education plan, which follows their particular learning pattern and style.

Born and raised in Poulsbo, WA, Kristina Halvorson began her musical education at the age of three with piano lessons then violin lessons at age eight. She began teaching younger students while under the tutelage of her instructors at age 15. She studied under two different private instructors, Lisa Moseng and Marie Morris, until she moved to Newberg, OR to further her education at George Fox University.

While at University, Kristina studied mathematics, but she did not let go of her passion for music. She furthered her musical knowledge in Piano and voice through private instruction.
